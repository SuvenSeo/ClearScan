ClearScan is a free, local-first document scanner for Android.

Scan receipts, contracts, IDs, and notes with your camera. Everything stays on your device — no account, no ads, and no forced cloud upload.

FEATURES
• ML Kit document scanning with PDF and JPEG output
• Local OCR with searchable PDF export
• Merge, split, rotate, sign, redact, and password-protect PDFs
• Folders, tags, favorites, and full-text search
• Optional biometric vault lock
• Encrypted storage and passphrase-protected backups
• Self-host export to WebDAV or paperless-ngx
• Sinhala and Tamil OCR (beta — accuracy still being benchmarked)

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
ClearScan does not include analytics or ad SDKs. OCR runs on-device. Network access is used only when you enable self-host export or check for app updates.

ClearScan is open source. Built for people who want CamScanner-style tools without subscriptions or watermarks.
